Get in touch

Here are a few things to know before booking us for your special day...

  • Our musicians play acoustic instruments meaning there is no amplification. Most venues do not require a sound system. However, if you should need one it can be provided for an additional fee.
  • If your event is outside: A covered or shaded area MUST BE PROVIDED as wooden instruments cannot be exposed to direct sunlight or moisture.
  • If the temperatures are predicted to be below 60° at the time of your ceremony floor and overhead heaters must be provided. We cannot play outside if temperatures are below 45 degrees.
  • In regards to music selection: Special requests (when we have to order music) are included in your package price up to $50.00. Any orders exceeding $50.00 will be added to your final balance.
  • We do not attend rehearsals due to the performance schedule of our musicians. We arrive one hour prior to the ceremony giving us ample time to speak with the coordinator. A representative can be at the rehearsal for a fee of $100.00.
  • Musicians will arrive one hour prior to the ceremony.
  • Client must provide chairs for string group
  • The average ceremony is 20-30 minutes. When you book with us our team reserves 2 hours to be on-site at your ceremony. Additional hours are billed at $50/hr. per player.

Frequently Asked Questions

DO YOU NEED A DEPOSIT?

Yes. We do require a 50% non-refundable deposit and contract to hold your date and time.

DO I HAVE TO PICK ALL THE MUSIC FOR THE CEREMONY AND PRELUDE?

Our guidelines are to pick one song for each of the following:

  • Seating of the grandparents/mothers
  • Bridal Party (groomsmen and bridesmaids - often referred to as the processional)
  • Bride's Entrance
  • Unity Candle/Special Music (optional)
  • Recessional (after you're pronounced Mr. and Mrs.)

WHAT WILL YOU WEAR?

Our musicians wear all black. The men typically wear tuxedos or black suit coats.
