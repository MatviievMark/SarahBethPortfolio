About her

Meet Sarah Beth Mullen, a seasoned musician with a profound affinity for the cello. Renowned as a dedicated instructor of string and piano, she not only performs masterfully but also imparts her extensive knowledge to budding musicians.

Sarah Beth's expertise further extends to the meticulous care and restoration of string instruments, exemplifying her unwavering commitment to the art of music.

Notably, she completed an internship under the mentorship of Mark Benischek at StringWorks in Illinois during the summer of 2023.

Embark on a harmonious journey with Sarah Beth, where each note resonates with a harmonious blend of passion and skill, or commence your own musical journey under her guidance.
